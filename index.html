Privacy & Data Security

🔒 Your privacy is guaranteed:

  • All AI models run locally in your browser
  • Camera images never leave your device
  • Voice queries are processed offline
  • No cloud APIs or external servers
  • No tracking or analytics
  • Models are cached in IndexedDB

📊 Data Storage:

  • Models: ~475MB (downloaded once)
  • Settings: Local browser storage
  • No data uploaded anywhere

How to Use Vision Assistant

🎯 Auto Mode

Press "Start Auto Mode" for continuous scene analysis. The assistant will describe what it sees every few seconds, helping you navigate your environment.

🎤 Query Mode

Press "Ask Question" to speak a question about your surroundings. Examples:

  • "What's in front of me?"
  • "Is there any obstacle?"
  • "What color is this?"
  • "Can you read this text?"

🔊 Audio Controls

Use "Stop Audio" to interrupt speech at any time.

📱 Accessibility

All buttons are large and work with screen readers. The interface uses high contrast colors for better visibility.

⚡ Performance Tips

  • Ensure good lighting for better detection
  • Keep device steady when asking questions
  • Models need ~500MB free space
  • Chrome 124+ recommended for best performance
